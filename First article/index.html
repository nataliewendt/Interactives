Harry Styles teases first album since 2022

By Natalie Wendt

Harry Styles is teasing a new album with billboards around the world. This will be his fourth studio album, after his 2022 Grammy-winning album, “Harry’s House.”

The 31-year-old singer has been hinting at a comeback since publishing a YouTube video on December 27 titled, “Forever, forever.” The video shows footage from Styles’ final show of his 2023 tour, ending with the message, “We belong together.”

On Monday morning, posters with this phrase were seen in cities including:

  • New York City
  • Palermo
  • São Paulo
  • Berlin
