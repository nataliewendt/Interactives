Who is Olivia Dean? Get to know the Grammy Award's 2026 best new artist

By Natalie Wendt

26-year-old British singer Olivia Dean capped off her already extraordinary breakout year by winning the Grammy Award for best new artist in Los Angeles on Sunday.

The category was stacked with other rising stars like Alex Warren, Addison Rae, and Leon Thomas. Chappell Roan, who won the category last year, presented Dean with her award.

In August, Dean became the first British female artist to achieve three simultaneous Top 10 hits since Adele in 2021, with "Man I Need" and "Nice to Each Other," as well as "Rein Me In," where she is featured with Sam Fender.
